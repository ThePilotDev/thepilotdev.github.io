About Me

Hello! My name is Jose G. Duarte N, also known as Pilot. I’m a dedicated Minecraft developer from Venezuela with 3 years of experience specializing in addons, maps, and dynamic worlds for the Minecraft Bedrock Marketplace. My work involves a strong technical understanding of Minecraft Bedrock Edition, combined with extensive skills in JavaScript and TypeScript to maximize the use of the Minecraft Bedrock scripting API.

Over the years, I have developed a solid foundation in version control with Git, ensuring efficient collaboration and smooth project management. My projects, available on the Minecraft Marketplace, are crafted with a deep understanding of Minecraft commands, functions, and technical artistry. I am also familiar with project management tools like GitHub Issues and Jira, which help keep my workflow organized and efficient.

Beyond development, I have a background in technical art, focusing on asset implementation and optimization, and have also created custom tools to streamline the development process. I am always open to new challenges, whether freelance or as part of a team, and I bring both dedication and flexibility to my work. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to working together to create something amazing!

My Approach

  • Creative Design: Crafting unique, visually appealing worlds and interactive experiences.
  • Technical Precision: Developing optimized code and efficient structures for smooth gameplay.
  • Player-First Mindset: Designing with player engagement and immersion at the forefront.
  • Constant Evolution: Always learning new techniques to enhance each project.
